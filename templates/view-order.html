View Your Order

Due to our strict privacy protocols, we do not maintain a user dashboard. Here is how to locate your purchase details.

Check Your Email

SecureAttic uses a "Fire & Forget" delivery system. Immediately upon successful payment, your License Key and download link are dispatched to the email address you provided.

We do not store your order history in a web portal accessible via login. This ensures that even if our website were compromised, there is no database of user accounts to leak.

Step 01

Search Your Inbox

Search for emails from Lemon Squeezy (our merchant of record) or subject lines containing "SecureAttic License".

Step 02

Check Spam Folder

Automated transactional emails can sometimes be flagged. Please check your Junk/Spam folder if the email does not arrive within 5 minutes.

Still can't find it?

You can recover your purchase history directly through our payment processor using your email address.
