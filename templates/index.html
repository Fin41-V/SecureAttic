Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I lose my USB drive?

Don't worry. During setup, you are given a 12-word "Seed Phrase" (BIP-39 Standard). You can use this phrase to regenerate your key on a new USB drive. Your data is never permanently lost unless you lose both the drive AND the seed phrase.

Does this work with any USB drive?

Yes. SecureAttic works with standard USB flash drives, YubiKeys, or even external SSDs. We fingerprint the device's hardware serial number to create the unique binding.

Can IT or forensic tools see my files?

With Ghost Mode active, your files masquerade as system files (e.g., nt_log.sys). Without the key to decrypt the file header, they appear as corrupted or binary system data, often ignored by standard scans.
