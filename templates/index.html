Operational Flow

From Setup to Stealth.

1. Select Targets

Choose specific folders on your Windows machine that contain sensitive IP, financials, or personal assets.

2. Bind Hardware

Plug in your physical USB key. SecureAttic generates a unique hardware hash that becomes the "Master Key."

3. Activate Sentry

Remove the USB. Your files instantly vanish and encrypt. Re-insert to unlock the vault. No key, no data.

Technical Stack

Architecture.

Hardware-Rooted Trust

Encryption keys are never stored on your disk. They are derived in real-time from the unique hardware serial of your authorized USB.

Kernel-Level Monitoring

Our Windows driver monitors device mount points at the kernel level for sub-100ms response times during device removal.

Non-Resident Memory

The application wipes its own memory footprint every 60 seconds to prevent cold-boot memory forensics.
