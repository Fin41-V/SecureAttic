The Security Lifecycle

Automated protection from binding to lockout.

1. Hardware Binding

We calculate a hash from your USB's unique serial number. Your key only exists when this specific device is plugged in.

2. Active Sentry

A silent background process polls your ports every 3 seconds. Drive removed? The vault locks instantly.

Locked files are renamed to system drivers like nt_driver.sys. To forensics, it looks like OS noise.
