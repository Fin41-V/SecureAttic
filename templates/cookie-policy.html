Cookie Policy.

Effective: Feb 10, 2026 Revision: 3.0.0-Titan

SecureAttic operates primarily as a desktop application with minimal web dependencies. We distinguish between "Browser Cookies" (used on our website) and "Local State" (used by the desktop app).

1

Desktop Application

The SecureAttic desktop executable does not use tracking cookies. Instead, it uses local JSON configuration files stored securely in your user profile: %APPDATA%\SecureAttic to maintain your settings and license activation state.

2

Essential Web Cookies

When visiting our marketing website or using the checkout portal, essential cookies are required. These allow you to purchase a license and securely process payments. Without these, the checkout flow cannot function.

3

Embedded Webview

The desktop application utilizes a "Webview" (embedded browser) to render the user interface. This component may cache local UI assets (HTML/CSS) to improve performance, but it does not track user behavior or send analytics data to third parties.

4

Third-Party Processors

Our payment provider, Lemon Squeezy, acts as the Merchant of Record. They may set cookies during the checkout process to detect fraud and validate payment sessions. These are governed by Lemon Squeezy's own privacy policy.
