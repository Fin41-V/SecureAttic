Cookie Policy

Last Updated: Jan 25, 2026

SecureAttic uses cookies and similar technologies to ensure our website and SaaS license portal function correctly. We do not use cookies for behavioral tracking or advertising.

1. Essential Cookies

These are necessary for the website to function. They allow you to log into your license dashboard and remain authenticated during your session.

2. Analytical Cookies

We use anonymous analytical tools to understand how visitors interact with our landing page. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our payment provider, Lemon Squeezy, may set cookies required to process your transaction and prevent fraud during the checkout process.

4. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. However, disabling essential cookies may prevent you from accessing your license management dashboard.
