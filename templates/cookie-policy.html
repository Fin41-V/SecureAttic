Cookie Policy

Transparency regarding data persistence in our desktop application and web portal.

Last Updated: Oct 10, 2026

SecureAttic operates primarily as a desktop application with minimal web dependencies. We distinguish between "Browser Cookies" (used on our website) and "Local State" (used by the app).

1. Desktop Application

The SecureAttic desktop application (executable) does not use cookies. Instead, it uses local JSON configuration files stored in %APPDATA%\SecureAttic to maintain your settings, license activation state, and vault preferences.

2. Essential Web Cookies

When visiting our marketing website or using the checkout portal, essential cookies are required. These allow you to purchase a license and securely process payments. Without these, the checkout flow cannot function.

3. Embedded Webview

The desktop application utilizes a "Webview" (embedded browser) to render the user interface. This component may cache local UI assets (HTML/CSS) to improve performance, but it does not track user behavior or send analytics data.

4. Third-Party Processors

Our payment provider, Lemon Squeezy, acts as the Merchant of Record. They may set cookies during the checkout process to detect fraud and validate payment sessions. These are governed by Lemon Squeezy's privacy policy.
