Privacy Policy.

Effective: Feb 10, 2026 Revision: 3.0.0-Titan

At SecureAttic, privacy isn't just compliance—it's architecture. We operate on a Local-First, Zero-Knowledge basis. We technically cannot see, access, or sell your data because it never leaves your physical device.

Core Architecture

The Developer of SecureAttic does not collect telemetry, usage statistics, or file metadata. The only network request made by this application is a one-time verification of your license key.

1

Data We Do Not Collect

To be explicitly clear, the SecureAttic application has NO capability to transmit:

  • Your files, folder names, or directory structures.
  • Your original hardware serial numbers (we only store a non-reversible hash locally).
  • Your Master Keys or Recovery Phrase. We cannot recover lost keys.
2

Data We Do Collect

Our data collection is limited strictly to transaction processing:

  • License Key: Sent to our API to verify validity.
  • IP Address: Logged temporarily during activation for fraud prevention (preventing one key from being used on 100 devices).
3

Local Data Storage

All configuration data is stored locally in your user profile: %APPDATA%\SecureAttic

  • vault_targets.json (Protected paths)
  • usb_registry.json (Hardware mapping)
  • ms_d3d_info.bin (Encrypted validation token)
  • recovery_vault.bin (Local key backup)
4

Processors

We use a single trusted third-party provider:

  • LemonSqueezy: Acts as our Merchant of Record. They handle all credit card processing, tax compliance, and invoicing. SecureAttic never sees or stores your financial information.
