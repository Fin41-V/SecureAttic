Privacy Policy.

Effective: Feb 05, 2026 Revision: 1.0.2

At SecureAttic, privacy isn't just a compliance requirementâ€”it's the core engineering principle. We operate on a Local-First, Zero-Knowledge architecture. This means we technically cannot see, access, or sell your data because it never leaves your physical device.

Core Promise

The Developer of SecureAttic does not collect telemetry, usage statistics, or file metadata from your local installation. The only network request made by the application is a one-time verification of your license key.

1

Data We Do Not Collect

To be explicitly clear, the SecureAttic application has NO capability to transmit:

  • Your files, documents, or directory structures.
  • Your hardware serial numbers (used strictly for local hashing).
  • Your encryption keys or recovery seeds.
2

Data We Do Collect

Our data collection is limited strictly to transaction processing and license validation.

  • Billing Info: When you purchase a license, your payment data is processed by LemonSqueezy (Merchant of Record). SecureAttic never sees your credit card number.
  • License Verification: During setup, the app sends your License Key to the LemonSqueezy API to verify validity.
  • Email Address: Used solely to deliver your license key and critical security updates.
3

Local Storage

The application creates a local registry file (vault_targets.json) on your machine to remember which folders are protected. This file is stored on your hard drive and is never uploaded to any server. If you delete the application, these local records are destroyed.

4

Third-Party Processors

We use a limited set of trusted third-party providers:

  • LemonSqueezy: Used for payment processing, tax compliance, and license key generation.
