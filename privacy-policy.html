Privacy Protocol

Privacy Policy.

Updated: Jan 25, 2026 Zero-Trace Commitment

At SecureAttic, our architecture is built on "Privacy by Design." We prioritize the local sovereignty of your data. This policy explains the minimal telemetry required to maintain your SaaS subscription and our absolute refusal to touch your personal data.

The Non-Collection Pledge

Because SecureAttic is a hardware-locked local vault, we explicitly do not collect, view, or transmit:

  • Encrypted file contents
  • Master Passwords
  • Folder Structures
  • Hardware Serial Keys

1. Information We Process

To provide the SaaS experience, we only process the following data points:

  • Account Identity: Your email address and billing metadata provided via our payment processor, Lemon Squeezy.
  • License Verification: An anonymous unique identifier used to verify that your Sentry monitoring subscription is active.
  • System Health: Anonymous crash reports and Windows versioning data used to prevent OS-level kernel conflicts.

2. Purpose of Processing

We use your minimal information solely to manage your access to the software, process renewals, and provide localized technical support. We do not engage in data mining, ad-tracking, or behavioral profiling.

3. Data Sovereignty

Your vault data exists exclusively on your physical hardware. If you choose to uninstall SecureAttic and wipe your local directory, all associated encrypted data is purged from the universe. We maintain no server-side shadows or cloud backups of your files.

4. Infrastructure Partners

SecureAttic partners with Lemon Squeezy for secure payment processing. They handle your financial credentials according to PCI-DSS standards. SecureAttic never sees or stores your credit card details.

5. Your Global Rights

Regardless of your location, we honor the principles of the GDPR and CCPA. You have the right to request a full export of your account metadata or the immediate deletion of your subscription profile. To exercise these rights, initiate a request via the contact below.
