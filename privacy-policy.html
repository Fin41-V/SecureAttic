At SecureAttic, our architecture is "Privacy by Design." We prioritize the local sovereignty of your data. This policy explains how we handle the minimal information required to run our SaaS platform.

1. Data We Never Collect

Because SecureAttic is a hardware-locked local vault, we explicitly do not collect, view, or store:

  • Your encrypted files or folder structures.
  • Your encryption keys or Master Passwords.
  • Your physical hardware hashes or USB serial numbers.
  • Any metadata regarding your vault activity.

2. Information We Collect

To provide the Service, we only collect the following:

  • Account Information: Email address and billing details provided via our payment processor (Lemon Squeezy).
  • License Status: A unique anonymous identifier to verify your active SaaS subscription.
  • Technical Logs: Anonymous crash reports and OS versioning to improve software stability on Windows.

3. Use of Information

We use your information solely to manage your subscription, provide technical support, and ensure compliance with our Terms of Service.

4. Data Sovereignty

Your vault data exists only on your local machine. If you uninstall SecureAttic and delete your vault, all associated encrypted data is permanently removed from your device. We maintain no cloud backups of your files.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Lemon Squeezy for payment processing. They maintain their own privacy policies regarding your financial data. SecureAttic does not store credit card numbers on its own servers.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR in the UK/EU), you have the right to access, correct, or delete your account information. Contact us at privacy@secureattic.com to exercise these rights.
